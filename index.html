Mammals

Mammals are distinguished from other vertebrate animals by several unique features. All mammals produce and secrete milk from mammary glands to feed their offspring.

Invertebrates

Invertebrates are animals without a backbone or bony skeleton. They range in size from microscopic mites and almost invisible flies to giant squid with soccer-ball-size eyes.

Prehistoric

Prehistoric animals lived more than 5,000 years ago before people could read or write. Explore the incredible world of dinosaurs through fascinating facts, and the latest science.

Amphibians

Amphibians are cold-blooded vertebrates (vertebrates have backbones) that don’t have scales. They live part of their lives in water and part on land.

Read more

Reptiles

Reptiles are cold-blooded vertebrates. They have dry skin covered with scales or bony plates and usually lay soft-shelled eggs.

Birds

Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates and are the only animals with feathers. Although all birds have wings, a few species can't fly.

About Us

Nusantara Geographic adalah website yang menampilkan keindahan fauna dan kekayaan satwa dari Indonesia maupun luar Indonesia. Melalui artikel, foto, dan video, website ini mengajak pembaca untuk mempelajari satwa satwa menakjubkan, langka, dan menjaga keanekaragaman satwa .
